# AI4Life Learning — Brand & Offering Overview

---

## What is AI4Life?

**AI4Life Learning** is Sri Lanka's dedicated AI literacy and education platform built to make Artificial Intelligence understandable, practical, and accessible to everyone — regardless of their technical background.

At a time when AI is reshaping every industry, most education platforms still cater only to developers and data scientists. AI4Life was created to close that gap — empowering professionals, students, entrepreneurs, and everyday people to understand, work alongside, and benefit from AI tools confidently.

> **Mission:** To make AI education practical, inclusive, and transformative for non-technical Sri Lankans and beyond.

> **Vision:** A future where every professional — regardless of their field — understands AI well enough to use it, lead with it, and grow because of it.

---

## What AI4Life Offers

### 1. AI Literacy Course
**AI Literacy — Foundation Course for Non-Tech Professionals**

The flagship programme designed from the ground up for people with no coding or technical background. It covers:

- What Artificial Intelligence actually is (cutting through the hype)
- How AI tools work and where they're used in the real world
- Practical AI tools you can use immediately in your job or business
- Understanding AI ethics, bias, and responsible usage
- How to communicate about AI confidently in professional settings
- Prompting skills — how to get the best results from AI assistants

**Who it's for:**
- Business professionals and managers
- Entrepreneurs and startup founders
- Marketing, HR, finance, and operations teams
- Government and public sector employees
- Anyone curious about AI but unsure where to start

---

### 2. Certified Completion
Upon successful completion of any AI4Life course, learners receive a **digitally verifiable certificate** with a unique Certificate ID.

- Certificates are publicly verifiable at [ai4life.lk/verify](verify.html)
- Employers and institutions can confirm authenticity instantly
- Each certificate is linked to the learner's name, course, and issue date

---

### The Learning Experience

1. **Enroll** — Connect with the AI4Life team via WhatsApp to register for the current or upcoming batch
2. **Learn** — Access course material through structured modules with real-world examples and demonstrations
3. **Practice** — Hands-on exercises using actual AI tools (no software installation required for most activities)
4. **Complete** — Finish all modules and assessments to qualify for certification
5. **Certify** — Receive your verified digital certificate with a unique ID
6. **Apply** — Immediately use new skills in your workplace, business, or studies

---

### Enrollment Process

Enrollment is handled directly through **WhatsApp** for a personal, guided experience:

1. Click the **Enroll via WhatsApp** button on the website
2. A pre-filled message is sent to the AI4Life team
3. The team responds with batch dates, pricing, and registration details
4. Payment and confirmation are completed over WhatsApp or email
5. Access to course materials is granted upon confirmation
